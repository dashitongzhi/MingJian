# Strategic Decision Support

This context names the public decision workflow that turns evidence, simulation, and structured disagreement into recommendations that remain inspectable over time.

## Language

**Debate**:
A structured exchange in which assigned roles critique evidence and revise positions before a decision recommendation is finalized.
_Avoid_: Chat, discussion, panel

**Debate Target**:
The single run, claim, scenario branch, or report whose conclusion is being challenged.
_Avoid_: Subject, resource, target object

**Debate Adjudication**:
The evaluation that turns completed debate rounds into a verdict, decisive evidence, conditions, risks, alternatives, and a conclusion.
_Avoid_: Scoring, summarization

**Verdict**:
The adjudicated disposition of a debate: accepted, rejected, or conditional.
_Avoid_: Answer, result, status

**Structured Dissent**:
The preserved minority position, including its claims, evidence gaps, confidence trajectory, and monitoring recommendations.
_Avoid_: Negative feedback, losing argument

**Debate Observation**:
A typed progress fact emitted while a debate is being conducted, such as a completed round or final verdict.
_Avoid_: Raw event, callback payload
